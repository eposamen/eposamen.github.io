about

Welcome! My name is Emma Posamentier, and I am currently a senior studying computer science engineering and studio art at Tufts University. I love making things – whether it be a beautiful website, a painting, or a photograph, seeing something come to life before my eyes one step at a time is my passion.

Throughout my college career I have coupled my engineer-focused classes with my passion for the arts by enrolling in at least one art class each semester, and as a result I will be graduating with a minor in studio art with a focus in watercolor painting. I believe that this broad range of passions makes me a better and more creative computer programmer, as well as a more interesting person.

Having created art my entire life, my introduction to computer science was eye opening. Code became a new medium for me to use to carry out an idea. It allows me to merge my creativity with technical puzzles in order to create a product that is not only visually appealing, but is also useful.

At my past internships I have worked full stack, and am currently proficient in Javascript, PHP, HTML, CSS, and SQL queries as well as having 6 years of experience with Adobe Photoshop. I am hoping to secure a position after I graduate where I can commingle my twin passions for creativity and science.

If you have any questions, feel free to email me at eposamen@gmail.com or call me at 281-825-7521.
